19) The convergence criteria for the Newton-Raphson in subroutine NEWRAX (line 292 of butil.f) does not properly account for the limitations of numerical precision. In our tests it failed
for BrCl in dark conditions.  The code fails to converge to the desired precision of 1.0E-10 as there is insufficient numerical precision. Curiously the strategy of shortening the time gap
in subroutine NEWRAF to improve convergence makes the matter worse!
The C++ code has implemented an additional achievable convergence criteria based upon the numerical precision (EPSILON) of the machine and uses the higher limit of the desired and achievable.
